Our Journey

Our relationship has been a long journey filled with love, mistakes, lessons, and endless moments where we chose each other again and again. We didn’t come this far because things were perfect we came this far because we never gave up on each other no matter how many times we fell apart. We’ve had days full of laughter, days full of tears, days where we felt lost, and days where just hearing each other’s voice made everything feel right again. We argue, we misunderstand each other, we get emotional and hurt, but at the end of the day the love we share always pulls us back, because deep down we both know we’re meant for each other.

Us

We do so much for each other without even realizing it. We stay up late when the other is sad. We explain the same things over and over until the other feels okay. We try to change small habits so the other doesn’t get hurt again. We apologize, we heal, we forgive, and we grow. We talk about our future, we dream together, we get scared together, we protect each other, and we hold on to each other even through the hardest storms. We love each other in our own imperfect ways sometimes loudly, sometimes silently, sometimes clumsily but always deeply and honestly.

Our Promise

What makes our relationship special is that nothing between us is fake. The emotions are real, the pain is real, the love is real, and the connection is something that doesn’t happen twice in a lifetime. We are two imperfect people who fight, break, fix, love, and come back stronger every single time. And no matter how many ups and downs come our way, we still choose each other with the same heart, same love, and same madness that we had from the start. Because at the end of the day, she is my person and I am hers and that’s something I cherish every single day.
